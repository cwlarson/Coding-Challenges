Gumroad Coding Challenge

How the game works:

  • Letters appear in the box below at a certain interval
  • All existing letters will move to the right
  • Hit a letter on the keyboard to clear the oldest instance of that letter, and to earn yourself 1 point
  • Careful! Hitting a letter on your keyboard that isn't in the box will lose you a point!
  • If any letter makes it all the way to the right of the box, you lose!
  • Press the 'escape' key to quit the game
